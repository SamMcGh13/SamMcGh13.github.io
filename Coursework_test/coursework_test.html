I'm stuck in a website, get me out of here!

Welcome to "I'm stuck in a website, get me out of here!" the hit game you've never heard of!

You are a web technician who is doing routine checks of all the sites the company you work for uses but you notice the site is being altered in real time, there's a person stuck in this website!
Only you can get them out! There is no clear explaination as to why this person is stuck inside of this website, but you're the only one who has access to the site, you have to save him before it's too late.
